Description

At a glance Farm In Every Home



Our product is for them who has a small or large place in their balcony, rooftop or front yard to plant small plants of vegetables or flowers as their hobby or as a small business as well as for them who wants to do research on different plants that means, for almost everyone.

The main goal of our product is to help non-farmer people to build IOT based small nursery in their home or office by controlling nursery from any internet connected devices and ensuring a green and healthy environment everywhere with the advantage of ensuring food security for the society.

Features

Features Of Our Project

  • IOT Based Nursery Module

    Advanced technology, as we want to make your hobby lot easier. This IoT based nursery module helps you to monitor and have control of the maintenance of your nursery from any place.

  • Smart User Interface

    Smart user interface that makes your hobby an interesting part of your life. Now you can take care of your plants like playing a video game. Web apps helps you to use the interface from any device.

  • Real time data Monitoring

    Real time data monitoring, analysis and saving them to a database is the most beautiful part of this module which helps you to get suggestion in your device about future production and necessary steps for your farming.

  • Portable and light weight

    Portable and light weight, itâ€™s easy to move to anyplace just like your trolley bag.

  • Flexible

    Flexibility, which is our main focus while designing our product for the city dwellers like you, so that you can have two or three slot and can enlarge as much as necessary for you according to your free space.

  • Low Bandwidth Consumption

    Runs with much lower bandwidth, so you can even use it where data connection is slow. No need to worry about bulk internet bills.

  • Low Power Consumption

    Inexpensive and consumes less power, that saves your money and suitable for good profit in business purpose.

  • User Friendly

    Easy to use, anyone can use the device. So, place it in your suitable place, plug it in and enjoy the blessing of modern technology, have fresh foods, grow beautiful flowers, decorate your home and office and be a part of a healthy ecosystem.
