Farm In Every Home



Statement Of The Problem





In recent years house and industries are being built on agricultural lands which is affecting our food security. If this continues, after few decades there will be no land left for farming. But as we cannot stop building houses, so we took step to create farming lands in our houses. Here comes the main problem. People nowadays donâ€™t prefer farming as their occupation. Though some persons try to build nursery in their houses as a hobby they face following problems

1. Time
2. Proper knowledge of farming

Finally safe and healthy foods are being replaced by unhealthy and poisonous foods which are being produced by our farmers for increasing the production. Shortage of labor in farming is also a big problem now. Besides, poor farmers cannot afford advanced technology in their lands.



Consumers



Our product is for them who has a small or large place in their balcony, rooftop or front yard to plant small plants of vegetables or flowers as their hobby or as a small business as well as for them who wants to do research on different plants that means, for almost everyone. The main goal of our product is to help non-farmer people to build small nursery in their home or office and ensuring a green and healthy environment everywhere with the advantage of ensuring food security for the society.



Vision





In future, when there will not be enough lands left for farming, people will start thinking about farming on the moon or Mars. But if we start farming in every smallest places in smarter way with the help of our advanced technology, then we can ensure our food security as well as build green and healthy environment in cities.

Mission



Our mission is to build technology for the future world where our consumers can have safe and healthy food cultivated by themselves. We also ensure our customers extra profit spending a little amount of time. Our products help people utilize their full potential with less labor and time. We work with IoT technology for mass people and we want to lead this sector as well. As a team of technicians we believe in working with most recent and advanced technologies and spreading them to the world.
